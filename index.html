Genevieve Holmes

Project and Team Manager

For the past three years, I worked as a project manager and team manager for Dimension Data AG & Co. KG, an IT consulting firm in Munich, Germany. I carried out large-scale telecommunications projects in both Germany and Switzerland and led the major accounts team at Dimension Data.

I have grown up in a musical family, been a pianist and accompanist throughout my life and earned my BA in Music at Guilford College in Greensboro, NC. While at Boosey & Hawkes, Inc. in New York City, I thoroughly enjoyed working with living composers on pre-publication scores, organizing Boosey's perusal score library and being exposed to great modern music.

While in Munich, I continued to pursue my passion for music as a volunteer. In 2011, I became a founding board member of English-Speaking Music Ensembles, e.V. ESME is a non-profit organization consisting of over 200 musicians from all over the world involved in our orchestral, choral and big band ensembles. I filled the board role of Artistic Manager and managed our musical vision through the selection, procurement and online cataloguing of our repertoire among other daily operations. ESME brings a unique concert experience to Munich, performing music of many different genres, periods and styles.

I love working with people from different countries and cultures. I speak English and German fluently and am trying to brush up on my French. I also love to travel, row and swim.
